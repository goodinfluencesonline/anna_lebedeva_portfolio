CoinFlip
Lifecycle Architecture & Retention Go-To-Market Strategy

CoinFlip

The Challenge

Building a fully automated communication flow between the business and customers to drive long-term retention, while simultaneously identifying viable user segments and testing new channels for acquisition.

The Strategy

Designed an end-to-end digital footprint for the user journey. Built a Salesforce-integrated lifecycle engine (Onboarding, Abandoned Cart, First Transaction) alongside a highly commercial, gamified SMS promotional calendar. Conducted deep customer development to segment users, which allowed for targeted VIP advocacy programs—successfully maintaining retention and revenue even during sudden regulatory market shifts. Tested and scaled new acquisition channels, from physical event activations (Cryptocon) to targeted B2B LinkedIn outreach for the OTC desk.

The Impact

Drove a 75% increase in monthly acquisition, a 20% increase in MoM retention, and generated a $400K+ B2B pipeline by aligning automated communications with user behavior.

iSpring Solutions
Go-To-Market Strategy & Localization User-Focused Campaign Strategy

iSpring Solutions

The Challenge

Penetrating the established AU/NZ market with a global e-learning product, requiring deep research to identify viable new market opportunities and pathways for localized adoption.

The Strategy

Conducted extensive market mapping to uncover a unique local friction point: Australia’s strict RTO (Registered Training Organisation) certification requirements. Built a tailored Go-To-Market ecosystem around this opportunity, utilizing targeted lead magnets, multi-channel webinar funnels, and strategic message placement to meet users early in their compliance journey.

The Impact

Translated a market gap into a sustainable revenue stream, achieving a 14% YoY increase in regional sales and a 19% increase in MQLs.

BP & Wild Bean Cafe
User-Focused Campaign Strategy Marketing Leadership

BP & Wild Bean Cafe

The Challenge

Managing national promotional cycles, commercially driving promotional performance, and modernizing the customer experience across a massive corporate network of 1,400+ locations.

The Strategy

Handled the commercial mechanics behind the creative, including complex vendor negotiations, cost structuring, and distribution comms for the Wild Bean Cafe network. Bridged the gap between head-office strategy and retail execution by managing the physical and digital launch of “Couchfood” (UberEats integration). Continuously optimized promotional cycles to drive bottom-line performance while executing high-visibility initiatives, including BP’s first influencer marketing campaign (BP Run) and a national COVID-19 healthcare worker initiative.

The Impact

Drove a 17% increase in cafe sales, spiked brand search terms by 324%, and successfully integrated digital convenience models into traditional retail environments.
