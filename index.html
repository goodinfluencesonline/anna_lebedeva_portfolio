CoinFlip

The Challenge

Building a fully automated communication flow between the business and customers to drive long-term retention, while simultaneously identifying viable user segments and testing new channels for acquisition.

The Strategy

Designed an end-to-end digital footprint for the user journey. iSpring Solutions

The Challenge

Penetrating the established AU/NZ market with a global e-learning product, requiring deep research to identify viable new market opportunities and pathways for localized adoption.

The Strategy

Conducted extensive market mapping to uncover a unique local friction point: Australia’s strict RTO (Registered Training Organisation) certification requirements. Built a tailored Go-To-Market ecosystem around this opportunity, utilizing targeted lead magnets, multi-channel webinar funnels, and strategic message placement to meet users early in their compliance journey.

The Impact

Translated a market gap into a sustainable revenue stream, achieving a 14% YoY increase in regional sales and a 19% increase in MQLs.
